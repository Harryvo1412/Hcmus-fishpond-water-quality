# Pilot Fieldwork – Sampling & In-Situ Measurements (Rounds 1–3)
**Date:** 2025-12-14  
**Location:** HCMUS Fishpond  
**Session:** Pilot sampling + in-situ measurements  
**Samples:** Sample01–Sample03  
**Rounds:** Round 1, Round 2, Round 3 (R1–R3)

## Purpose
This folder documents the **first pilot fieldwork session** of the HCMUS fishpond water quality project. The photos provide **transparent evidence** of (i) sampling and labeling at collection and (ii) in-situ measurements across three repeated rounds.

## Contents
### A) Field sampling & labeling
- `...EquipmentAndSamples...` : equipment and sampling setup
- `...FieldSampling...` : sampling action at the pond
- `...LabelledAtCollection...` : label verification at collection time
- `...all_samples_overview...` : overview of all collected samples

### B) Measurement evidence (R1–R3)
For each sample (Sample01–Sample03), photos capture device screens for:
- **pH**: `...pH_Round1`, `...pH_Round2`, `...pH_Round3`
- **EC/Temperature**: `...EC-Temp_Round1`, `...EC-Temp_Round2`, `...EC-Temp_Round3`

## QA / Notes
- Photos are used for **traceability and reproducibility** (screen evidence + labeling), not as the primary numeric dataset.
- All numeric values are recorded in: `data/measurements/2025-12-14_pilot_measurements.xlsx`
